# Customer Research

## Objective

Understand target customers to inform product development and marketing strategy.

---

## Primary Research Plan

### Customer Interviews (Target: 10-15 interviews)

**Segment 1: Small Business Owners**
- Questions:
  - What tools do you currently use to manage your business?
  - What's your biggest operational challenge?
  - How much do you spend on software/tools monthly?
  - What would make you switch to a new solution?

**Segment 2: Individual Professionals/Freelancers**
- Questions:
  - What tasks take up most of your time?
  - What automation/tools do you use?
  - What's missing from current solutions?
  - What's your budget for productivity tools?

**Segment 3: E-commerce Buyers**
- Questions:
  - Where do you typically shop online?
  - What influences your purchase decisions?
  - What product categories interest you most?
  - What's your average order value?

---

## Secondary Research

### Market Trends (China)

**SaaS Market:**
- Growing acceptance of paid software
- Preference for mobile-first solutions
- Integration with WeChat ecosystem critical

**E-commerce:**
- Social commerce rising (Douyin, Little Red Book)
- Live streaming commerce mainstream
- Consumer preference for value + quality

---

## Hypotheses to Validate

1. **B2B SaaS**: Small businesses willing to pay 200-500 RMB/month for tools that save 5+ hours/week

2. **E-commerce**: Consumers looking for [specific category] at mid-tier pricing

3. **Distribution**: WeChat + content marketing most cost-effective for customer acquisition

## Personas (Draft)

### Persona 1: "Efficient Eddie" - Small Business Owner
- **Demographics**: 35-45, owns 10-50 person business
- **Goals**: Reduce costs, improve efficiency
- **Pain Points**: Too many tools, no integration
- **Budget**: 500-2000 RMB/month for software
- **Channels**: Industry forums, peer recommendations

### Persona 2: "Freelancer Fiona"
- **Demographics**: 25-35, independent professional
- **Goals**: Maximize billable time, look professional
- **Pain Points**: Administrative overhead
- **Budget**: 100-300 RMB/month
- **Channels**: Social media, content marketing
