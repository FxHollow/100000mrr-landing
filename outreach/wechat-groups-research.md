# WeChat Groups & Communities - Target Research

## Objective
Identify and join 5-10 relevant WeChat groups and online communities for AI/LLM integration consulting lead generation.

---

## Target Segments

### 1. SME Business Owners
**Where to find:**
- Industry association WeChat groups
- Local business chamber groups
- Entrepreneur networking groups

**Search Keywords:**
- 中小企业交流群 (SME Exchange Group)
- 创业者联盟 (Entrepreneurs Alliance)
- 老板圈子 (Boss Network)

### 2. E-commerce Operators
**Where to find:**
- Taobao/Tmall seller groups
- Cross-border e-commerce communities
- Social commerce groups

**Search Keywords:**
- 淘宝卖家交流群 (Taobao Seller Exchange)
- 跨境电商群 (Cross-border E-commerce)
- 电商运营群 (E-commerce Operations)

### 3. Tech/Startup Community
**Where to find:**
- Startup incubator groups
- Tech founder networks
- Product manager communities

**Search Keywords:**
- 创业者技术群 (Startup Tech Group)
- 产品经理交流群 (PM Exchange)
- 互联网创业群 (Internet Startup)

## Action Plan

### Week 1: Join Groups
- [ ] Search WeChat for relevant groups using keywords above
- [ ] Ask existing network for introductions to 2-3 groups
- [ ] Join 5 groups minimum
- [ ] Observe group dynamics before posting

### Week 2: Establish Presence
- [ ] Share first article in 2-3 most active groups
- [ ] Answer 3-5 questions related to AI/automation
- [ ] Build relationships with group admins
- [ ] Track engagement metrics

## Group Engagement Rules

### DO:
- Provide value before promoting
- Answer questions genuinely
- Share actionable insights
- Build relationships with admins
- Track which groups drive engagement

### DON'T:
- Spam links immediately after joining
- Hard sell in every post
- Ignore group rules
- Post without reading room temperature
- Forget to follow up with interested members
