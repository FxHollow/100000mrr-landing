# Marketing Strategy - 100000MRR

## Executive Summary

**Goal**: Support company revenue target of 100k RMB/month through strategic marketing and customer acquisition.

**Business Model**: Information services (SaaS, apps) + E-commerce operations

---

## Market Analysis

### Target Segments

1. **B2B - Small/Medium Businesses**
   - Need: Efficient tools for operations, customer management
   - Willing to pay: Subscription model (99-999 RMB/month)
   - Decision factors: ROI, ease of use, integration

2. **B2B - Individual Professionals/Agents**
   - Need: Productivity tools, automation
   - Willing to pay: Lower tier (29-199 RMB/month)
   - Decision factors: Time savings, features

3. **B2C - E-commerce Consumers**
   - Need: Quality products at good value
   - Purchase behavior: Price sensitive, trend-driven
   - Channels: Social commerce, marketplaces

## Revenue Model Assumptions

To reach **100k RMB/month**:

**Scenario A - SaaS Focus:**
- 500 customers × 200 RMB/month = 100k
- Or: 200 customers × 500 RMB/month = 100k

**Scenario B - E-commerce Focus:**
- 2000 orders × 50 RMB margin = 100k

**Scenario C - Hybrid:**
- SaaS: 50k (250 customers × 200 RMB)
- E-commerce: 50k (1000 orders × 50 RMB)
