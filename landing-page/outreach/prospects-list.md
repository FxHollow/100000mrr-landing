## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: SME Business Owners
- **Revenue**: ¥10M-100M annually
- **Employees**: 20-200 people
- **Pain Points**: High operational costs, manual processes, customer service overload
- **Budget**: ¥30k-80k for AI integration projects
- **Decision Speed**: Fast (owner-operator or small leadership team)

### Secondary ICP: E-commerce Operators
- **Platform**: Taobao/Tmall/JD with ¥5M+ GMV
- **Pain Points**: Customer service volume, inventory management, content creation
- **Budget**: ¥20k-50k for automation solutions
- **Decision Speed**: Medium (need to see clear ROI)

### Tertiary ICP: Tech Startups
- **Stage**: Series A or profitable bootstrap
- **Need**: MVP development, AI feature integration
- **Budget**: ¥50k-150k for custom development
- **Decision Speed**: Fast (technical founders understand value)

## Conversation Scripts

### Discovery Call Opening (30 min)
```
1. Introduction (2 min)
   - Thanks for taking the call
   - Brief background on 100000MRR

2. Their Situation (10 min)
   - "Tell me about your current operations..."
   - "What's your biggest challenge right now?"
   - "How are you handling [specific process] today?"

3. Pain Exploration (8 min)
   - "How much time does this take weekly?"
   - "What would solving this be worth to you?"
   - "What happens if this doesn't get solved?"

4. Solution Overview (5 min)
   - Brief overview of relevant AI approach
   - Share 1-2 similar case examples
   - Outline 4-week process

5. Next Steps (5 min)
   - Offer detailed assessment call
   - Discuss timeline and investment range
   - Send follow-up materials
```

### Objection Handling

**"We don't have the budget"**
- "I understand. Many of our clients started with similar concerns.
   The key is that AI integration typically pays for itself in 3-6 months.
   Can we explore what ROI would look like for your specific case?"

**"We tried AI before and it didn't work"**
- "That's not uncommon. AI has improved dramatically in the last 12 months.
   What specifically didn't work before? I'd be happy to give an honest
   assessment of whether it makes sense today."

**"We need to think about it"**
- "Of course. What specific questions do you have?
   Happy to address any concerns before you decide."
