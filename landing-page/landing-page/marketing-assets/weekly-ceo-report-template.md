## Weekly Rhythm

### Friday Morning (30 min)
1. Pull metrics from all platforms
2. Update CRM with current status
3. Calculate conversion rates
4. Fill in weekly report template
5. Send to CEO via WeChat/email

### Friday Afternoon (CEO Review)
- CEO reviews report
- Discusses blockers/asks
- Aligns on next week priorities
